David Rubenstein Distinguished Professor of Economics, Duke University.

Research Associate at NBER; Research Fellow at CEPR (Climate Change & Environment); senior fellow at ABFER; research affiliate with IGC.

Co-editor, American Economic Journal: Micro. Associate editor, RAND Journal of Economics. Previously edited Review of Economics and Statistics (2021–2024) and Journal of Development Economics (2018–2021).
